About ZenPomo

ZenPomo is a productivity extension designed to enhance your work or study sessions with a combination of time management and music. It integrates a Pomodoro timer technique with a compact Spotify mini web player, creating an immersive and focused work environment.

Features

  • Pomodoro Timer: Utilize the Pomodoro technique to boost your productivity. Set work intervals, take short breaks, and stay on track with your tasks.
  • Block Feature: Enhance your productivity by blocking distracting websites or apps during your work sessions. Stay focused on your tasks without interruptions.
  • Spotify Mini Web Player: Enjoy your favorite music from Spotify without leaving your work environment. Control playback, change tracks, and set the perfect soundtrack for your focused sessions.

Current Version: 1.0
